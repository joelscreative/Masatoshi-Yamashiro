PORTFOLIO

Masatoshi Yamashiro was born in Toyama, Japan, in 1980.

He Started learning photography when he was 20 at Santa Monica College in Los Angeles, Ca. After he graduated from college, he moved to New York and studied photo journalism and portrait photography at the School of Visual Arts.

He started his Career shooting celebrities and artists back in Los Angeles in 2004. He lives in Tokyo Japan now.
